Past Projects

Mini Sumobot - World RoboGames, April 2013

Our first robotic competition entry consisted of a 500g autonomous Mini Sumobot for the World RoboGames in San Francisco CA.

Micromouse- Brown IEEE Robotics Olympiad, April 2013

In this competition, a small robot must autonomously navigate and solve a complex maze within a strict time limit. The robot with the lowest run time from the start square to the center of the maze wins. Competitors from universities such as Harvard, Brown, and Princeton brought fierce competition. We were awarded Best Design Award by a panel that included the dean of Brown's School of Engineering.
